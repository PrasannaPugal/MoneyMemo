Help & Guide

📋 What is MoneyMemo?

MoneyMemo is a personal ledger to track money you've sent to or received from people. It works entirely in your browser — no account needed, no data leaves your device.

👤 Profiles

  • A profile represents a person (e.g. Dad, Friend, Vendor).
  • Type a name and tap + Add to create one.
  • Tap the ✎ pencil icon on a card to rename it.
  • Tap the 🗑 bin icon to delete (with undo).
  • Duplicate names are blocked automatically.

💰 Transactions

  • Tap a profile card to open its transaction ledger.
  • Sent = money you gave them (they owe you).
  • Received = money they gave you (reduces what they owe).
  • The balance shown is: total sent − total received.
  • Use the date picker to backdate a transaction.
  • Tap ✎ to edit any transaction (type, amount, note, date).
  • Tap 🗑 to delete — an Undo button appears for 5 seconds.

🔍 Search, Filter & Sort

  • Search — type in the search box to filter transactions by note.
  • Filter — use the dropdown to show only Sent or Received.
  • Sort — tap the Date button to toggle newest-first / oldest-first.

✅ Settle Up

Tap the Settle button in a profile's header to zero out the balance. This adds a single offsetting transaction automatically.

💾 Data Management

  • Export — downloads all data as a data.json file.
  • Import — loads data from a previously exported JSON file.
  • Reset — restores the built-in seed data (with confirmation).
  • CSV — inside a profile, tap CSV to export that profile's transactions as a spreadsheet-friendly file.

🌙 Dark Mode

Tap the moon/sun icon in the top-right corner to toggle dark mode. Your preference is saved automatically.

🔒 Privacy

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Clearing browser data will erase everything — use Export to keep a backup.
